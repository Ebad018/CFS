# **Game Design Document: Relationship & Social Systems**

Project: Cultivation Fortress

Scope: NPC Social Web, Lineage/Genetics, Romance, and the "Revenge Loop."

Core Philosophy: "Hit the Young, the Old Come." Actions have multi-generational consequences.

---

## **1\. Technical Architecture (The "Guanxi" Web)**

Relationships are not just a single number; they are a directed graph connecting entities.

### **1.1 Data Structure (The Relation Component)**

Every NPC and Player has a SocialComponent.

C++  
struct Relationship {  
    int target\_entity\_id;  
    int affinity;           // Range: \-100 (Nemesis) to \+100 (Soulmate)  
    int intimacy;           // Range: 0 to 100 (Knowledge of each other)  
    Enum type;              // NONE, PARENT, CHILD, SPOUSE, MASTER, DISCIPLE  
    vector\<string\> history; // Log: "Killed my brother in Year 402."  
};

### **1.2 The "Karma Network" (Propagation Logic)**

When the Player interacts with Target A, the engine checks Target A's network.

* **Example:** Player kills *Disciple Wang*.  
* **Propagation:**  
  1. *Wang's Father (Elder)*: Affinity \-100 (gains "Vendetta").  
  2. *Wang's Master (Sect Leader)*: Affinity \-50 (gains "Dislike").  
  3. *Wang's Rival*: Affinity \+20 (gains "Amused").

## **2\. Social Dynamics: Friendship & Romance**

### **2.1 Affinity Tiers**

| Value | Status | Gameplay Effect |
| :---- | :---- | :---- |
| **\+80 to \+100** | **Taoist Companion / Soulmate** | Shared Inventory. Will die to protect you. Unlocks "Dual Cultivation." |
| **\+50 to \+79** | **Close Friend / Sworn Sibling** | Will join your Party/Squad for free. Shares secrets (Map info). |
| **\+10 to \+49** | **Acquaintance** | Will trade fairly. Polite greetings. |
| **\-10 to \+9** | **Stranger** | Neutral. |
| **\-11 to \-49** | **Disliked** | Refuses trade. Badmouths you to others (lowers Reputation). |
| **\-50 to \-99** | **Enemy / Rival** | Will challenge you to duels. Bids against you in Auctions. |
| **\-100** | **Nemesis (Blood Feud)** | Will hunt you actively. Ignores zones/rules to kill you. |

### **2.2 Romance & Marriage**

* **Trigger:** Affinity \> 80 \+ Successful "Proposal" (Requires Charisma check or Gift).  
* **Dual Cultivation:** A special "Seclusion" mode available only to spouses.  
  * *Effect:* XP Gain x1.5 for both.  
  * *Bonus:* If Spouse has a special constitution (e.g., RACE\_02 Phoenix Scion), Player gains temporary Elemental Resistance.  
* **Daoist Wedding:** A generic event that invites high-affinity NPCs. Generates massive Reputation.

## **3\. Lineage System (Children & Genetics)**

The ultimate goal of the "Legacy" system. Children are new Entities generated by mixing the parents' data.

### **3.1 Conception & Birth**

* **Trigger:** Chance event during "Dual Cultivation" or via specific Items (HRB\_027 Nirvana Seed used as fertility aid).  
* **Time Skip:** Pregnancy lasts 9 months (Engine Ticks).  
* **Birth:** A new Entity spawns in the "Clan" location.

### **3.2 Genetic Inheritance Logic**

The child inherits stats based on a weighted formula \+ Mutation Chance.

1. **Spirit Root (Talent):**  
   * *Logic:* (ParentA.Root \+ ParentB.Root) / 2 \+ Random(-10 to \+10).  
   * *Mutation:* 1% chance to spawn a "Heavenly Root" (Max Talent) regardless of parents.  
2. **Bloodlines (Traits):**  
   * If Parent is RACE\_02 (Phoenix) and other is RACE\_01 (Human), child has 50% chance to be RACE\_02 or Half-Blood (Human with Fire Affinity).  
3. **Appearance:** Names are pulled from the Father's Surname (e.g., "Ye").

### **3.3 Raising the Heir**

* **Age 0-10:** Idle state. Consumes Resources.  
* **Age 10 (Spirit Awakening):** Player must assign a **Master**.  
  * *Choice:* Teach them yourself (Relationship+) or send to a Sect (Better stats, Relationship-).  
* **Succession:** If Player dies, they can choose to **Possess** the child, continuing the game with the Child's stats/inventory but the Player's memories (Soul).

## **4\. Hostility System: Rivals & Grudges**

This creates narrative tension without scripted quests.

### **4.1 The "Young Master" Loop (Procedural Arrogance)**

1. **The Trigger:** Player offends an NPC with the Arrogant personality tag (e.g., winning an Auction item).  
2. **The Escalation:**  
   * *Stage 1:* Verbal Warning ("Do you know who my father is?").  
   * *Stage 2:* Assault (Sends hired thugs/Outer Disciples).  
   * *Stage 3:* Duel (The Young Master attacks directly).  
3. **The Climax:**  
   * *If Player Spares:* Young Master gains Humiliated trait. Trains 2x speed. Returns in 10 years as a Boss.  
   * *If Player Kills:* Triggers **"Call the Elders"**.

### **4.2 "Call the Elders" (Recursive Aggro)**

When a "Protected" NPC (Disciple/Child) is killed:

* **Notification:** "You feel a terrifying killing intent lock onto you from 5,000 miles away."  
* **Hunter Spawn:** The game spawns a high-level entity (Parent/Master).  
  * *Tracking:* They have the SKL\_UTL\_02 (Soul Search) ability to find the Player's location.  
  * *Resolution:* Kill the Elder \-\> Aggros the Ancestor \-\> Aggros the Sect Leader \-\> **Sect War**.

### **4.3 Rivalry Mechanics**

Rivals are different from Enemies. They want to *beat* you, not necessarily kill you.

* **Rubber Banding:** Rivals get a hidden XP multiplier to keep their Cultivation Realm close to the Player's.  
* **Interactions:**  
  * They appear in Tournaments to knock you out.  
  * They try to steal "Lucky Chances" (e.g., arriving at a Spirit Fruit node just before you).

## **5\. Master & Disciple (Mentorship)**

### **5.1 Becoming a Disciple**

* **Requirement:** Passing a Sect Entrance Exam or impressing a wandering Elder.  
* **Benefit:**  
  * Monthly stipend (Spirit Stones).  
  * Access to the Master's "Manual Library" (Skills).  
  * Protection (Master will intervene if you are attacked by higher-tier NPCs).

### **5.2 Taking a Disciple**

* **Requirement:** Player Realm \> Golden Core.  
* **Action:** "Inspect Root Bone" on an NPC. If Talent is high, "Offer Mentorship."  
* **Gameplay Loop:**  
  * You must provide resources (ORE, HRB) to the disciple.  
  * Disciple acts as an automated minion (Gathering, Crafting).  
  * **Betrayal Risk:** If Loyalty drops (e.g., you starve them), they may steal your inventory and flee (becoming a "Traitor Disciple" event).

## **6\. UI Integration (Social Panel)**

A dedicated tab in the "Dashboard" GUI.

* **Tree View:** Visual Family Tree (Ancestors \-\> Parents \-\> Siblings \-\> Offspring).  
* **Network View:** A spider-web graph showing active relationships.  
  * *Green Line:* Friend.  
  * *Red Line:* Grudge (Thickness \= Hatred Level).  
  * *Blue Line:* Master/Student.  
* **Log:** "Social History"  
  * *"Year 405: Mo Long swore revenge on you."*  
  * *"Year 410: You married Ye Yan."*  
  * *"Year 412: Ye Yan gave birth to Ye Xia."*
